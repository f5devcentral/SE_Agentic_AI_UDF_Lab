City: Barcelona
Theme: Budget-friendly hotels for nature-oriented travelers
Season: Spring

1) GreenStay Barcelona Park Hotel
- Stars: 3
- Typical price per night (spring): 70–90 USD
- Description: A simple, clean hotel located near Parc de la Ciutadella and within walking distance of several metro lines. Rooms are modest but well-maintained, with basic amenities and some small balconies.
- Why it fits a nature lover: Quick access to parks and the waterfront. You can start your day with a short walk in the park before exploring other nature spots.
- Budget notes: For a 7-night stay, the total room cost can often be kept below 600 USD if booked early, leaving enough budget for transport and food under a 1000 USD cap.

2) Montjuïc Garden Inn
- Stars: 3
- Typical price per night (spring): 65–85 USD
- Description: Located on the slopes of Montjuïc, this inn offers small rooms with views over the city or the hill. It focuses on quiet evenings and early mornings, ideal for hikers and walkers.
- Why it fits a nature lover: You can walk directly into the Montjuïc parks and gardens without taking public transport. Sunset walks are literally at your doorstep.
- Budget notes: A one-week stay is realistically in the 500–580 USD range, giving extra room for a day trip to Montserrat and meals.

3) Coastal Breeze Budget Hotel
- Stars: 2
- Typical price per night (spring): 55–75 USD
- Description: Basic but reliable hotel near the beach promenade. Rooms are compact; the main advantage is location and price.
- Why it fits a nature lover: Easy access to long seaside walks, sunrise on the beach, and coastal cycling paths.
- Budget notes: One week can often be managed around 450–520 USD, ideal when combined with low-cost or free nature hikes inland.

4) Collserola Trail Lodge
- Stars: 2–3
- Typical price per night (spring): 60–80 USD
- Description: Small lodge near the foothills of Parc de Collserola, easily reachable by local train. Rooms are simple; some include shared kitchens for self-catering.
- Why it fits a nature lover: Great base for multiple hiking days in Collserola with minimal commute time.
- Budget notes: With self-catering and low nightly rates, it is one of the best options to keep the total under 1000 USD including basic meals and local transport.

Selection Strategy for Budget-Constrained Nature Trips
- For a 7-day trip with a total budget of 1000 USD:
  - Target hotel costs in the 450–600 USD range.
  - Prefer locations close to parks, hills, or the sea to minimize daily transport costs.
  - Combine at least one inland nature base (Collserola Trail Lodge) with a few nights near the waterfront (Coastal Breeze Budget Hotel) if splitting stays is acceptable.
