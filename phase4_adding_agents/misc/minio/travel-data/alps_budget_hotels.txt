Region: Alps (generic Alpine town)
Theme: Budget hotels and guesthouses for nature trips
Season: Spring shoulder season

1) Alpine Valley Guesthouse
- Stars: 2–3
- Typical price per night (spring): 60–85 USD
- Description: Family-run guesthouse slightly outside the town center, with simple rooms and shared kitchen access. Often popular with hikers and climbers.
- Why it fits a nature lover: Quick access to valley floor trails and forest paths, quieter than the main tourist streets.
- Budget notes: A 7-night stay can often be held between 420 and 560 USD, leaving enough budget for local trains, a cable car or two, and basic meals.

2) Mountain View Budget Hotel
- Stars: 2
- Typical price per night (spring): 55–75 USD
- Description: Basic hotel with small but clean rooms and mountain views from some windows. Limited extras; the focus is on a safe, inexpensive place to sleep.
- Why it fits a nature lover: You spend most of the day outside; the hotel is just a base camp for hiking and exploring.
- Budget notes: Works well for strict budgets; combine with supermarket meals and free hiking routes.

3) Riverside Eco-Lodge
- Stars: 3
- Typical price per night (spring): 70–90 USD
- Description: Small eco-lodge located near a river, with an emphasis on sustainability. Some rooms include balconies and communal lounge spaces.
- Why it fits a nature lover: Waking up to the sound of water and birds, with trails starting directly from the lodge.
- Budget notes: Slightly more expensive than very basic options, but can still fit into a 1000 USD total if transport costs are moderate.

4) Hostel with Private Rooms
- Stars: 1–2
- Typical price per night (spring): 40–60 USD (private room)
- Description: Hostel-style accommodation that offers private rooms with shared bathrooms. Simple and functional.
- Why it fits a nature lover: Very budget-friendly; ideal if you plan to spend almost all your time outdoors.
- Budget notes: You can easily keep accommodation under 400 USD for a week, freeing budget for occasional higher-cost activities like cable cars or guided hikes.

Selection Strategy for a 1000 USD Budget
- Focus on 40–80 USD per night options.
- Choose locations close to trailheads or public transport to avoid extra costs.
- Prefer lodging with kitchen access to reduce meal costs over a full week.
